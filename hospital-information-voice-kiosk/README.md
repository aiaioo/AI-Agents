# Hospital Information System

## Healthcare Information Kiosk and Feedback System with Voice Support in Indian Languages

In many parts of India, patients who speak local languages alone cannot understand reports and prescription sheets, making compliance with treatment directives difficult.

When patients go to a health center, they want:
1. Availability of doctors
2. Quality of care
3. Information about services and locations of various doctors and rooms.
4. Cost-effectiveness
5. Assistance making sense of reports, doctors' notes and prescriptions.
6. Feedback and complaint registration

## Smart Kiosk for Hospitals

One solution is a Smart Kiosk for hospitals that is:
1. Voice activated
2. Answers questions orally in Indian languages
3. Provides information or records issues
4. Monitors facilities through its cameras
